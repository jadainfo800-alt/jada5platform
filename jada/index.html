Frequently Asked Questions

How does the referral program work?

Our referral program rewards you for bringing new users to Jada 5. When someone signs up using your referral link and makes their first deposit, you'll receive 8% of that deposit amount as a commission. For example, if your friend deposits 10,000 UGX, you'll earn 800 UGX in commission.

What does my friend get when they sign up?

Your friend will receive a 1,000 UGX bonus when they sign up using your referral link and verify their account. This bonus can be used to play any game on our platform.

When do I receive my referral commission?

Referral commissions are credited to your account automatically once your referred friend makes their first deposit. The funds will be available in your wallet immediately and can be used for ticket purchases or withdrawn.

Is there a limit to how many people I can refer?

No, there's no limit to the number of people you can refer. The more friends you bring to Jada 5, the more you can earn!

Can I withdraw my referral earnings?

Yes, referral earnings are added directly to your main wallet balance and can be withdrawn using any of our supported withdrawal methods.
